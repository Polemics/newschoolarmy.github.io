Our Apps

At New School Army, we’re Soldier-Activists dedicated to empowering the force. We build apps that cut through administrative red tape, help leaders lead more effectively, and improve overall Soldier quality of life. From specialized NCO tools to forms and documents managers, our solutions streamline daily tasks, enhance readiness, and ensure you spend more time focusing on your mission and your Soldiers.

Below is a selection of our current apps. Each one is designed with real-world feedback, continuously refined over time, and built on a foundation of trust, utility, and respect for your time and duties. Whether you’re a seasoned NCO or newly enlisted, our apps aim to give you the edge, freeing you to focus on what matters most.
